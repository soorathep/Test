**Batteries & Supercaps** has put our work on the Front Cover of issue 6/2026:
*From Molecule to Stack: A Cross-Scale Design Framework for Aqueous Organic Redox Flow
Batteries*, credited to Soorathep Kheawhom and co-workers.

![Batteries & Supercaps Front Cover, issue 6/2026]({{ site.baseurl }}/covers/Batteries_and_Supercaps_2026.jpg)

The title carries the argument: a molecule that looks good in a vial is not yet a stack that
works, and the two are usually designed by different people using different criteria. The
same reasoning runs through the [CTI framework]({{ site.baseurl }}/{{ site.home_url }}#cti)
this group works from.

All twelve of the group's covers are on the
[Recognition]({{ site.baseurl }}/{{ site.home_url }}#recognition) page.
